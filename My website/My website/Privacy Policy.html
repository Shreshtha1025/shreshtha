User Account Deletion and Restoration Policy

2. Account Restoration: If you change your mind and wish to-continue using our services, you may restore your account within this 30-day grace period. Simply log in as you normally would, and you'll be presented with an option to cancel the deletion request and restore your account. If you choose to restore, all your account details and associated data will remain intact.

3. Permanent Deletion: If you do not log in or take any action within the 30-day grace period after initiating a deletion request, your accountand all associated data will be permanently deleted from our systems.
